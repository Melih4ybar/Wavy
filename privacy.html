Wavy does not share user information with third parties.

Wavy may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and improve the game. However, these technologies do not collect or track personal information.

Users can contact us at with any concerns or questions regarding the Wavy game. Users have the right to review, update, or delete their personal information at any time.

6. Change Notifications

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated by posting the updated policy here.

Wavy is designed as a safe game for children under the age of 13. It does not collect any personal information or contain any advertising content.
